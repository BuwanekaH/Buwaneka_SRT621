Welcome to the Mobasery Library

This is a small Website that help our Library to help readers find specific books of interest. Users can further explore our website by going through the options in the Navigation Bar.

The Website consist of various different pages the user can explore.

  • Homepage: The Welcome page of the Library
  • Book List page: This page consist of the lists of books available in the Library
  • Book details page: This page consist of the details of each book
  • Contact Us page: The uses can find our contact information and the location of the Library
  • Survey page: Users can Volunteer to participate in our Survey

Please feel free to explore our website, and while you are here, Enjoy!
